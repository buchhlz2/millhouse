Ryan Ernst is a graduate of the University of Illinois. He has been with Millhouse since he was in high school and uses his expertise to develop the Millhouse brand as well as run the day to day operations. In the Millhouse circle Ernst is considered "the man with the plan" creating the operating structure of the company as well as developing relationships with clients. Having experience working with both startups as well as fortune 500 companies Ernst brings a unique skill set to Millhouse. Because Ernst's first passion is film, he does a great job of bridging the gap between creativity and the the bottom line. He allows, in his words, "Millhouse's Greatest Asset" it's creative department to flourish without worrying about the dollars and cents.
